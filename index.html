Welcome to GitHub Pages.

Below is a map of restaurants participating in Local Restaurant Week in Western New York, April 22-28, 2013.

Because of technical problems with the map, three participating locations are currently missing from it. Anyway they seem like great places to dine:

  • Campobello's Cucina Italiana (9334 Transit Road)
  • Samurai (9648 Transit Road)
  • Savor (28 Old Falls Street).

This unofficial map was generated on April 23 by a local eater of food. It may become out of date or contain other errors. It is not produced or endorsed by, or otherwise affiliated with Food Service Enablers or Local Restaurant Week.

Please address any questions, corrections, or other comments about this map to Jonathan Bona: jonathanbona+lrw@gmail.com
